About

Good Human provides HR support that’s easy to understand and easy to action. Whether you’re hiring your first employee, managing growth, or navigating a difficult situation, I help you do the right thing — with confidence and care.

How I work

  • Practical: clear next steps, not jargon.
  • Compliant: aligned to NZ employment law and best practice.
  • Commercial: realistic advice that fits your workplace.
  • Human: fair, respectful processes and communication.

Who I’m a good fit for

  • SMEs without in-house HR
  • Owner-operators and managers who need backup
  • Growing teams building better people processes
  • Employers who want to be both fair and firm
