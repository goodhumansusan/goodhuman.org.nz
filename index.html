About

People stuff can be straightforward right up until it isn’t. Good Human helps make it manageable. From day-to-day questions to more complex situations, support is designed to be easy to understand and easy to action.

Everything is grounded in New Zealand employment law and best practice, with advice that fits real workplaces. The goal is simple. Do the right thing, write it down properly, and keep things moving.

What to expect

  • Clear steps in plain language
  • Practical options that suit your team
  • Calm support through tricky moments
  • Solid documentation so nothing is left to guesswork

Who it’s for

  • SMEs without in-house HR
  • Owner-operators and managers who want backup
  • Teams growing or tidying up people processes
  • Employers who want to be fair and still get things done
