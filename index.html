About Good Human

Good Human is a Human Resources business based in Tauranga, New Zealand, providing practical, compliant, commercially focused HR support to small and medium-sized businesses.

Good Human operates on a user-pays model offering flexible HR services both locally and remotely across New Zealand.

Vision & Values

Good Human exists to help businesses be good employers and good humans. Our values include integrity, fairness, empathy, and practical excellence.

Who We Work With

  • Small to medium-sized businesses
  • Business owners without in-house HR
  • Growing teams navigating complexity
  • Employers who care about doing things properly
